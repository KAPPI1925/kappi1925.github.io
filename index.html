About

I am Kamalesh Kanna S, a professional with a strong academic foundation and versatile skills in agriculture, geospatial technologies, and creative media. I hold a B.Sc. (Hons) in Agriculture from Tamil Nadu Agricultural University (TNAU) and an M.Sc. (Ag.) in Remote Sensing and GIS from TNAU, Coimbatore.
